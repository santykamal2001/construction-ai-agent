=== EXECUTIVE SUMMARY ===
The SR-45 bridge over Millbrook Creek was inspected on January 15, 2024. The bridge is a 3-span continuous steel girder bridge built in 1987 with a total length of 210 feet. Overall condition rating: FAIR (Rating: 5/9).

=== STRUCTURAL FINDINGS ===

1. DECK CONDITION:
   - Multiple transverse cracks observed on the bridge deck surface (Sections 2 and 3)
   - Crack widths ranging from 0.5mm to 2.1mm
   - Delamination detected in northwest quadrant, approximately 15 sq ft area
   - Spalling near expansion joint at Station 2+45
   - Pothole 8 inches diameter at the center lane (Station 1+82)
   - Condition Rating: 5 (Fair)

2. SUPERSTRUCTURE:
   - Steel girders showing surface rust at mid-span (Girders G2, G3, G4)
   - No section loss detected at this time — monitoring required
   - Cross bracing intact and properly connected
   - Bearing pads at Pier 1: slight lateral displacement (0.5 inches — within tolerance)
   - Bearing pads at Pier 2: Good condition
   - Condition Rating: 5 (Fair)

3. SUBSTRUCTURE:
   - Pier 1 (North): Minor cracking on pier cap, horizontal crack at 18 inches below bearing seat. Crack sealed with epoxy in previous inspection (July 2023) — re-inspection shows crack has not propagated.
   - Pier 2 (South): Good condition, no cracking observed
   - Abutments: Wingwall at North Abutment shows 3/8 inch separation from abutment backwall — fill material visible — flagged for repair
   - Scour at Pier 1 footing: Streambed elevation checked — no new scour detected since last inspection
   - Condition Rating: 5 (Fair)

4. CHANNEL AND CHANNEL PROTECTION:
   - Creek bank erosion on east side of Pier 1 — approximately 4 feet of bank lost since July 2023 inspection
   - Riprap displaced downstream — needs replacement
   - No debris accumulation at waterline
   - Condition Rating: 4 (Poor) — ACTION REQUIRED

=== SAFETY ISSUES ===
- Bridge rail has two broken posts at Station 0+22 (south side) — PRIORITY REPAIR
- Warning: Load posting inspection required — current posting may need review based on deck deterioration

=== PREVIOUS INSPECTION COMPARISON ===
July 2023 Inspection (Inspector: Sarah Chen):
- Overall Rating was 6 (Satisfactory)
- Deck cracks noted but smaller (0.3mm-1.2mm)
- Girder rust was not present in July 2023
- Creek bank erosion has accelerated — 4 feet loss in 6 months

DETERIORATION TREND: Accelerating — recommend moving to quarterly inspection cycle.

=== RECOMMENDED ACTIONS ===
Priority 1 (Immediate — within 30 days):
  - Repair broken bridge rail posts (Safety hazard)
  - Install erosion control at east bank of Pier 1

Priority 2 (Within 6 months):
  - Mill and overlay deck surface — Sections 2 and 3
  - Clean and paint steel girders G2, G3, G4
  - Repair wingwall separation at North Abutment
  - Replace displaced riprap

Priority 3 (Within 1 year):
  - Full deck condition survey with GPR (Ground Penetrating Radar)
